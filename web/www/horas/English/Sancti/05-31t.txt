!Commemoration of S. Petronilla, Virgin
Ant. The kingdom of heaven is like unto a merchantman * seeking goodly pearls,~
who, when he had found one pearl of great price, went and sold all that he had,~
and bought it. (Alleluia.)
_
V. Grace is poured into thy lips. (Alleluia.)
R. Therefore God hath blessed thee for ever.
